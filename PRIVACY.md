# LyricVibe Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 2026_

LyricVibe is built to be privacy-first. Here is everything it does with data:

## What LyricVibe does

- Reads the song title, artist, album, and playback position from the music page you activate it on (YouTube Music, Spotify Web Player, SoundCloud, etc.). This happens only after you click the extension icon or use the keyboard shortcut on that tab.
- Sends the song title and artist name to public lyrics APIs (LRCLIB and, as a fallback, lyrics.ovh) to fetch lyrics.
- Stores your preferences (theme, text size, see-through mode, per-song sync offsets) and a temporary lyrics cache locally in your browser using chrome.storage. This data never leaves your device.

## What LyricVibe does NOT do

- No analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind
- No audio recording or capture
- No browsing history collection
- No personal data collection
- No accounts, sign-ups, or cookies
- No data sold or shared with third parties
- No remote servers operated by LyricVibe — the extension is 100% client-side

## Third-party services

When fetching lyrics, the song title/artist are sent over HTTPS to LRCLIB (lrclib.net) and lyrics.ovh (api.lyrics.ovh) as a fallback. These requests contain no identifying information beyond your IP address, which is inherent to any web request.
